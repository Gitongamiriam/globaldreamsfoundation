GLOBAL DREAMS FOUNDATION

Global Dreams Foundation is based in Nairobi.It states its mission is to direct more funding to effective charitable project in the community.To be able to give to those who need our help .To also help ones who have been denied access to medication .We empower the girl child in the community and as well as fight for the right of people.

ABOUT US

We are a national network of community foundation bringing together people and organisation that want to improve thier communities .Together our members with tier partners on the group touch almost every aspect of the African Community and supporting Women's micro enterprise projects. A passionate desire to help people of africa solve critical problems and build a better future for children and the whole community atlarge.

PROJECTS

  • Girls Education-helps an african girl go to school and,stay in school and succeed in life
  • Feeding Program-provides children from poor backgrounds to acquire a decent meal
  • Shelter Program-we build decent shelters for the poor and the elderly in rural areas
  • Medical Program-focuses on HIV/AIDS awareness and prevention sensitization in slums
